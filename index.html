About me

I'm a Senior Operational Researcher based in Cardiff, Wales, with over six years of experience applying mathematical modelling, statistical analysis, and machine learning to healthcare and health economics. I work at Health Economics and Outcomes Research Ltd, where my focus spans microsimulation modelling, metamodeling with neural networks, and systems dynamics for healthcare applications. My background is interdisciplinary: I hold an MSc in Operational Research and Data Science from Cardiff University, an MSc in Immunology from the University of Glasgow, and a BSc in Biomedical Science from Queen Mary University of London. I build interactive analytics dashboards with R Shiny, develop AI tools using local LLMs, and bring a research-oriented approach to every project — from clinical data analysis and health economic modelling to optimisation and predictive analytics. I am also working on advancing the business use of controlled and standardised AI within organisations.
